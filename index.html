About ENTREPIX 2025

ENTREPIX 2025 is a premier innovation and entrepreneurship event organized by the Sustainable Engineers Club at M.Kumarasamy College of Engineering. This event aims to foster creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit among students, providing them with a platform to showcase their innovative ideas focused on Sustainable Development and transform them into viable business solutions.

Join us for an exciting day of idea pitching, mentorship, and networking opportunities. Whether you're passionate about sustainable construction, green building technologies, eco-friendly infrastructure, renewable energy, or any innovative solution promoting sustainability, this is your chance to shine and make a real impact on our planet's future!

  • AI for Astrophysics, Cosmology, and High Energy Physics
  • AI-guided Molecular Design and Quantum Phenomena
